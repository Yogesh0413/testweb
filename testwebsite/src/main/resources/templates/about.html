Who We Are

More Than a Hotel —
A Way of Life

The Grand Horizon was founded on a single conviction: that true luxury is not about excess, but about the art of attention. Every suite, every meal, every interaction is shaped by an obsessive care for detail and a deep respect for the guest.

Nestled between the mountains and the sea, our resort offers a rare convergence of natural beauty and refined hospitality — a place where time slows, and the world feels very far away.

What We Believe

Our Guiding Principles

Four convictions that shape every decision we make and every experience we create.

✦

Crafted Simplicity

The most profound luxury is the absence of friction. Every element of your stay is designed to feel effortless, invisible, and inevitable.

◈

Rooted in Place

Our cuisine, décor, and spirit draw from the landscape around us. We are of this land, and we honour it in everything we do.

◎

Human Connection

Behind every service is a person who genuinely cares. We hire for warmth first, and train the rest. No script — just sincerity.

⬡

Sustainable Stewardship

We are custodians of this beautiful place. Our practices are guided by a deep responsibility to the environment and to future guests.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2004

The Vision

Eleanor Vance purchases the coastal estate and begins a decade-long restoration project, preserving the original architecture while reimagining every detail.

2012

Grand Opening

The Grand Horizon opens its doors with 24 rooms and a waitlist that stretches three months. The world takes notice almost immediately.

2015

Culinary Recognition

Chef Marcus Chen joins and the Horizon restaurant earns its first Michelin star within 18 months of opening.

2016

Serenity Spa Opens

Our award-winning wellness sanctuary opens its doors, quickly becoming the most sought-after spa destination on the coast.

2019

Five-Star Recognition

Awarded five stars by three major international hospitality organisations simultaneously — the first property in the region to do so.

2024

The Villa Collection

Launch of our private villa collection — six exclusive residences with private pools, personal butlers, and the most expansive views on the property.
